What is POT.CY cold store?

POT.CY stands for Product Of The City. In our cold store we stock iced treats with ingredients sourced from local producers, foraged or from food surplus.

Why are we doing this?

Because we believe urban food could play an important role in the future of food in the city and that nothing really beats an ice cold summer treat.
